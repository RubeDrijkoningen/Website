Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to our FAQ page! Below, you’ll find answers to some of the most common questions we receive. If you don’t see your question here, feel free to reach out to us through our Contact Us page.

Orders & Shipping

1. How do I place an order?

Placing an order is easy! Simply browse our store, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the steps to enter your shipping details and payment information, and you’re all set!

2. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including: •Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, etc.) •PayPal •Your life or soul

3. Do you offer international shipping?

No! We ship universally. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location.

4. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location and selected shipping method. Generally: •Domestic shipping: [4–12 Seconds] •International shipping: [1–2 Minutes] • Universal shipping [1-2 Hours]

5. Can I track my order?

No! Our times are very precise!

Returns & Refunds

6. What is your return policy?

We want you to be happy with your purchase! If you’re not satisfied, you can return most items within [7] days of receiving your order (unless you paid with your life or soul). Items must be in their original condition and packaging. Some exclusions apply—see our Return Policy for full details.

7. How do I start a return?

To start a return, please contact us at EverythingNMore@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return. We’ll guide you through the process.

8. When will I receive my refund?

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 2 business days. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method.

Products & Stock

9. The item I want is out of stock. Will it be restocked?

We try our best to restock popular items. If an item is out of stock, you can contact us for more information or cry about it.

10. Do you offer gift cards?

No.
